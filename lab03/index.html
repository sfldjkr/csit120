Your Name is a Japanese animated film that tells a beautiful story about love, time, and destiny. The visuals are stunning, and the soundtrack perfectly fits the emotional journey.

What I Liked

  1. Gorgeous animation and scenery
  2. Emotional and unique storyline
  3. Music by RADWIMPS

What I Liked

Avengers: Endgame is the epic conclusion to the Marvel Infinity Saga. It combines action, sacrifice, and teamwork in a way that brought fans together worldwide.
