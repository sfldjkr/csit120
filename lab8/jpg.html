JPG Format Example

About This Image

This photograph was captured from an airplane seat during my flight from Nepal. The sun is about to set, and its warm golden light illuminates the clouds below, creating a breathtaking view from above. The layers of clouds and the glow of the sunset make the scene peaceful and visually striking.

Why JPG Format?

JPG is ideal for this image because it contains a wide range of colors and smooth gradients, like the golden sunlight blending into the clouds. JPG compression efficiently preserves the subtle color transitions in the sky while keeping the file size reasonable for web use.
